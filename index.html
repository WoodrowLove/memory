How to Play

Welcome to the Memory Card Game! Here's how to play:

  • Press the "Reveal" button to reveal the cards briefly.
  • Click on any two cards to try and find the matching pair.
  • If you find the matching pair on the first attempt, you win!
  • If you don't match the pair after 2 attempts, you'll lose and have a chance to try again.
  • You can press "Reveal" only twice per round.
  • Enjoy yourself!.
